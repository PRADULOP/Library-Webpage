"Harry Potter and the Sorcerer's Stone" by J.K. Rowling

The first book in the iconic Harry Potter series follows an orphaned boy, Harry, who discovers on his 11th birthday that he is a wizard. He attends Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry, where he befriends Ron Weasley and Hermione Granger, and uncovers a hidden mystery involving his dark past. This magical journey introduces themes of friendship, bravery, and the eternal struggle between good and evil.
